Bio

I'm Imama Ali, a Computer Engineer/Scientist with 10 years of industry experience, specializing in Generative AI (text, audio, images, and video), 2D/3D computer vision, and machine learning. I completed my PhD at the University of Memphis(2009-2013) under the supervision of Dr. Eddie L. Jacobs. Throughout my career, I have tackled problems in detection, 3D reconstruction, registration, and natural language processing. I am passionate about geometric computer vision, NLP, and deep learning. My work has included 3D facial reconstruction, depth estimation, 2D computer vision, and Natural Language Processing. At NIO, I led part of the digital cockpit team to develop and deploy sentry mode in production and patented the algorithms. More recently, I have been working on personal preojects found on github. Lip sync based on text, audio, and image modalities. In addition designing and building evaluation frameworks and metrics for LLM/LVM-based applications. My goal is to lead teams in achieving business objectives and advancing technology.
