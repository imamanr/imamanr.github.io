Imama Ali

Bio

Imama Ali is a Computer Engineer/Scientist with 10 years of industry experience, specializing in Generative AI(text, audio, images and video), 2D/3D computer vision, and machine learning. She completed her PhD from the University of Memphis(2009-2013) under the supervision of Dr. Eddie L. Jacobs. She has tackled problems in detection, 3D reconstruction, registration, and natural language processing. She is passionate about geometric computer vision, NLP, and deep learning. She has worked on 3D facial reconstruction, depth estimation, 2D computer vision, and Natural Language Processing. At NIO, she led part of digital cockpit team to develop and deploy sentry mode in production and patented the algorithms. Recently, at FlawlessAI, she worked on integrating text, audio, and images modalities for lip sync applications, in addition design and build Evaluation framework and metrics for LLM/LVM based applications. Her goal is to guide teams in achieving business objectives and advancing technology.
