Imama Ali

Bio

Imama Ali is a computer Engineer/Scientist, with 10 years of industry experience and specialization in the areas of Generative AI(Text, Audio, Images and Video), 2D/3D computer vision and machine learning. She earned her PhD from University of Memphis in 2013 and has been working in the industry for over 10 years now. She has worked on problems ranging from Detection, 3D Reconstruction, Registration and language modeling. She is passionate about problems in the area of Geometric Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. During her career, she worked on 3D facial reconstruction/modeling, depth estimation, 2D computer vision and intention classification tasks. During my time at NIO digital cockpit team, I had an opportunity to lead a team of 5-6 people for development and deployment of sentry mode features in production. Most recently, I have been working with different modalities involving text and audio along with images for lip sync application. My interest and aspiration lies in guiding teams towards achieving business objectives and fostering technical advancement.
